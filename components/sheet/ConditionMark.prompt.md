"Deprived" label + tickbox, as at the right end of the sheet's Name bar.

```jsx
<ConditionMark />
<ConditionMark checked />
<ConditionMark showIcon size={56} />
```

Pass `checked` to fill the box once a PC becomes Deprived (lacking food, water or rest). `showIcon` adds the kneeling-figure illustration — off by default to match the printout, where the bar carries only the word and the box.
