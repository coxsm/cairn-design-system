1px gray border, white fill, --radius-sm, edge nicks indenting inward, fatigue tickbox upper-right. No drop shadow.

Every box on the sheet — name bar, inventory rows, stat boxes, notes panel, rules frame — is a thin gray rule with slight rounding (--radius-sm for rows/boxes, --radius-md for large panels). Containment is drawn with a fine pen, never simulated with light. Nick rule: edge nicks always indent inward toward the card — both pointing right on a left edge (inventory rows), both pointing down on a top edge (stat boxes). Large panels use diamond notches at top/bottom center instead.
