Back — Rules Summary

STR: Brawn, prowess & resistance. 0 = Death.

DEX: Dodging, sneaking & reflexes. 0 = Paralysis.

WIL: Persuasion, intimidation & magic. 0 = Delirium.

SAVES
•Roll a d20 equal to or under an ability.
•1 is always a success, 20 is always a failure.
COMBAT

Initiative begins with a DEX save — those who succeed act before the enemies, then turns alternate back and forth. The lowest roll goes first, then clockwise around the table.

Attacks always hit. When hit with damage, subtract your Armor, and apply the remainder to your HP, then STR if HP reaches 0.

Damage → Armor → HP → STR
ACTIONS

On your turn, a character may move up to 40ft and take up to one action. Actions may include casting a spell, attacking, making a second move, or other reasonable activities. Drinking a potion is a free action.

HEALING
•HP is lost during combat & recovered instantly after combat ends.
•STR takes healing potions, spells, or time to recover.
INVENTORY

PCs have 10 inventory slots. Most items take up a one slot, but smaller items can be bundled together. Bulky items take up two slots and are awkward or difficult to carry.

SPELLBOOKS

Anyone can cast a spell with a WIL save. If you fail, the spell works but you gain 1 Fatigue (takes up an inventory slot). Spellbooks each hold one spell and take up one slot.
